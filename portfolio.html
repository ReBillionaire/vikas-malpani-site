Investment Philosophy

The thesis behind every check I write.

Founder-First

I back exceptional people, not pitch decks. I look for founders obsessed with the problem, not the fundraise. The best founders I’ve backed could talk about their problem for hours without checking the time.

India-Scale Problems

Technology solving problems unique to India’s scale: real estate, education, enterprise AI, and emerging sectors. India has 1.4 billion people and most industries still run on spreadsheets and phone calls.

Early & Active

Seed and pre-seed. I don’t just invest — I roll up my sleeves. Strategy, hiring, product, GTM. I’ve built four companies and I bring that operating experience to every investment.

Portfolio Companies

The companies I’ve backed, and the stories behind them.

Unicorn

Unacademy

Earliest Angel Investor — Dec 2015, $586K round

$3.4B+ valuation

When I met the founder, he was running a YouTube channel teaching coding. No app. No revenue. No team beyond himself. But when he talked about making quality education accessible to every student in India, I could hear the obsession. I wrote the check alongside Sujeet Kumar (Flipkart), Aprameya Radhakrishna (Taxi4Sure), Phanindra Sama (RedBus), and my CommonFloor co-founder Sumit Jain. Unacademy became a unicorn in 2020. Today, it’s one of India’s most valuable ed-tech companies with millions of learners.

Unicorn

Quikr

Via CommonFloor Merger — Jan 2016, $200M exit

Created classifieds leader

The CommonFloor-Quikr merger created India’s largest classifieds unicorn. What started as a real estate platform with 5 lakh+ listings across 200+ cities merged with Quikr to become India’s go-to place for buying, selling, and discovering everything from apartments to used cars to services. Tiger Global backed both companies and recognized the strategic value in combining real estate expertise with horizontal classifieds reach. This was one of India’s largest tech exits at the time.

Growing

NxtWave

Pre-Series A Investor — Nov 2021, $2.8M round

NxtWave is building the next generation of tech talent in India through structured, outcome-driven coding education. The founders have the same obsession about education access that I saw at Unacademy. They’re focused on making career transitions possible for anyone willing to commit to learning. Strong product-founder fit and a mission that resonates across millions of aspiring engineers across India.

Growing

Phyllo

Creator Economy Infrastructure

Creator economy infrastructure — APIs that connect platforms and unlock creator data. As someone who built Leher, a live audio-video social platform, I understood the pain of fragmented creator data firsthand. Phyllo solves a real problem: creators are scattered across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, and dozens of other platforms. Phyllo gives them and creators a unified view of their reach and monetization across every channel.

Growing

SeekAxis Technologies

Angel Investor — Jun 2022

Solving hiring challenges with technology. Good hiring is the single hardest problem every founder faces — I’ve experienced it at every company I’ve built. Finding the right people, evaluating them fairly, and building teams that scale is where many startups struggle the most. SeekAxis is building the infrastructure to make hiring faster, smarter, and more equitable.

Active

FiveS Digital

Strategic Advisor

Digital transformation for enterprises. The gap between where most Indian enterprises are and where they need to be is massive. Legacy systems, outdated processes, no data infrastructure. FiveS is bridging that gap by making digital transformation practical, affordable, and fast for mid-market companies that have been left behind.

Active

What I’m Looking For Now

Active investment areas and the next bets.

AI Infrastructure & Middleware

Building the layers that make AI useful for enterprises. At Garvik AI, we’re solving the knowledge and context problem that keeps AI from working in real production systems.

India Enterprise Software

B2B SaaS for Indian businesses. Most of India’s enterprise software market is dominated by global products built for different markets. There’s a massive opportunity for founders building software specifically for Indian enterprises.

AI + Education Intersection

Personalized learning at scale. AI can finally make education truly accessible and adaptive to every student’s learning style. I’ve seen what the Unacademy team built; I’m excited to see what the next generation of AI-powered education looks like.

Climate Tech for India

India-specific climate solutions. Not generic climate tech. Solutions that solve environmental problems unique to India’s geography, agriculture, and industry.

If you’re building in these spaces, I’d love to hear from you. Not your polished pitch deck — tell me about the problem you’re solving and why you’re the right person to solve it.
